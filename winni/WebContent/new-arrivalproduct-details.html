Care Instructions

  • First, remove the bouquet wrapper.
  • To keep the flower fresh for a long time, cut the stems one or two inches from the bottom.
  • Pick a vase and put water in it.
  • To avoid the growth of the bacteria, remove the leaves from below the water line.
  • To keep the bloom fresh for a long time, mix two teaspoons of sugar and lemon juice in the water.
  • Place the vase far away from direct sunlight.
  • Store the cake in a refrigerator.
  • Consume the cake within 24 hours.
